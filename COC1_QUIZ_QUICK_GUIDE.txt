================================================================================
  HOW TO USE
================================================================================

1. CLICK "TAKE A QUIZ"
   - Dashboard button

2. SELECT "COC 1: CONTINUATION"
   - Choose course

3. CLICK "START LEARNING"
   - See confirmation

4. CLICK "START QUIZ"
   - Quiz begins

5. READ QUESTION
   - Understand what's being asked

6. SELECT ANSWER
   - Click one of 4 choices (A, B, C, D)
   - Selected answer highlights in blue

7. SUBMIT ANSWER
   - Click "Submit Answer" button
   - OR wait 20 seconds (auto-submits)

8. SEE FEEDBACK
   - Correct: Green highlight + checkmark
   - Wrong: Red highlight + X + correct answer in green

9. CLICK NEXT QUESTION
   - Timer resets to 20 seconds
   - Continue to next question

10. REPEAT
    - Continue until all 29 questions done

11. VIEW PROGRESS
    - Click "Progress" tab
    - See your score
